How Safe Buy Realties Works

A structured, legally supervised workflow for sellers, buyers, tenants and professionals.

Supervised Real Estate, Step by Step

  • Vendors upload title documents, surveys and approvals for review.

1. Modus Operandi for Sellers

Sellers (owners or authorized agents) execute an online Power of Attorney appointing Safe Buy Realties Limited as the donee to:

  • Ascertain and describe the property and its survey details.
  • Secure buyers and perfect all steps necessary for sale.
  • Sell and alienate the property and remit sale proceeds to the seller’s designated account.
  • Authorize deduction of 10% brokerage/commission from source.

The Power of Attorney is irrevocable and signed, sealed and delivered by the Donor(s) in the presence of a witness.

Seller / Agent Information Collected

  • Full name, age, residential and office address
  • Phone number, email address
  • Valid ID (Driver’s Licence, National ID, PVC, International Passport)
  • Passport photograph

About the Property to be Sold or Leased

  • Size of land
  • Type of property
  • Full description and location
  • Owner’s name
  • Title documents available
  • Survey plan number
  • Final purchase price
  • Valuation report details

Documents Uploaded by Seller

  • Photocopy of survey plan
  • Photocopy of title documents (C of O, registered title)
  • Valuation report
  • Locational sketch
  • Purchase receipt
  • Property photos (front, rear, sides, interior, aerial if available)

2. Modus Operandi for Buyers

Once a buyer finds a desired property on the platform, the buyer also executes an online Power of Attorney appointing Safe Buy Realties Limited to:

  • Supervise the purchase of the selected property.
  • Secure and perfect all steps necessary for purchase.
  • Receive and disburse purchase price to the seller/owner.
  • Deduct 10% brokerage/commission from source.

Buyer Information Collected

  • Full name
  • Age
  • Residential address
  • Phone number
  • Email address

Details of Property Picked by the Buyer

  • Land size and property type
  • Full description and location
  • Owner’s name as seen on the website
  • Documents viewed on the website
  • Survey plan number viewed
  • Final purchase price
  • Buyer’s acceptance of price and valuation (via tick boxes)

Meeting with Safe Buy Realties Team

A meeting (physical or virtual) is scheduled between the buyer and Safe Buy Realties team to finalize instructions, timelines and next steps.

3. Steps to Take Before Buying

  • Conduct due diligence.
  • Search at the Land Registry.
  • Search at the Surveyor-General’s office to confirm area is not acquired.
  • Court search for pending suits.
  • Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) search (where applicable).
  • Physical inspection of the property.
  • Independent valuation by buyer (optional).

4. Steps After Meeting of Minds

  • Preparation and franking of title documents.
  • Payment and receipts.
  • Physical possession/hand-over.
  • Preparation of survey plan if required.
  • Perfection of title documents at the Land Registry.
